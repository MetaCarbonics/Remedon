Frequently Asked Questions

A quick guide to how Remedon works, what we support, and how organizations can engage with us.

1. What does Remedon do?

Remedon builds and delivers climate systems across carbon markets, GHG emissions accounting Scope 1 to 3, and circular economy compliance including Plastic EPR, enabling organizations to move from commitments to measurable and verifiable outcomes.

2. How is Remedon different from a consulting firm?

Unlike traditional advisory firms, Remedon focuses on execution and system delivery, including project development, compliance workflows, and monitoring frameworks that lead to auditable results rather than only reports. Our approach is strengthened by digital MRV systems that bring together remote sensing, geospatial analytics, and automated data pipelines for transparent and scalable verification.

3. What industries do you work with?

We work across sectors with significant emissions, resource use, or regulatory exposure, including manufacturing and heavy industry, FMCG and retail supply chains, infrastructure and energy, and public sector and government programs.

4. What is carbon asset development?

Carbon asset development involves converting climate activities such as afforestation, biochar, or waste management into verified carbon credits through structured project design, validation, and registry issuance.

5. Which carbon standards do you work with?

We align projects with leading global standards including Verra, Gold Standard, American Carbon Registry, and Climate Action Reserve, along with frameworks such as ART TREES and Plan Vivo Foundation. We also support eligibility under CORSIA and co benefits such as Climate, Community and Biodiversity Standards. Our approach remains registry agnostic so projects can align with additional or emerging standards based on their requirements.

6. Do you support Scope 3 emissions accounting?

Yes. We provide end to end Scope 3 value chain emissions analysis, including supplier mapping, logistics, product lifecycle impacts, and downstream emissions.

7. How do you ensure data accuracy and credibility?

Our approach is grounded in empirical analysis, validated methodologies, and structured MRV monitoring, reporting, and verification frameworks so outputs are audit ready and defensible.

8. What is Plastic EPR and how do you support it?

Plastic Extended Producer Responsibility requires organizations to manage plastic waste obligations. We support compliance planning, credit procurement and trading, and evidence traceability and reporting, aligned with India’s regulatory framework under the Central Pollution Control Board.

9. Do you provide end to end project execution?

Yes. We support the full lifecycle from feasibility and design to validation, issuance, compliance, and commercialization.

10. Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. Our solutions integrate with enterprise systems, supply chain data, and external datasets to enable seamless reporting and monitoring.

11. How long does it take to develop a carbon project?

Timelines vary by project type. Feasibility and design typically take two to four months, while validation and registration usually take six to twelve months.

12. What outcomes can we expect?

Outcomes may include verified carbon credits, Scope 1 to 3 emissions visibility, regulatory compliance for EPR and ESG reporting, and data backed decarbonization pathways.

13. Do you work with governments and public programs?

Yes. We support state and national programs, including large scale forestry, landscape restoration, and circular economy initiatives.

14. How do you price your offerings?

Pricing depends on the scope and structure of the engagement. We work across project based models for carbon development, subscription or system based models for MRV and emissions tracking, and transaction linked models for EPR and credit markets.

15. How do we get started?

We begin with a scoping and feasibility assessment to identify opportunities, compliance requirements, and implementation pathways.

16. Do you support Article 6 or compliance markets?

Yes. Where relevant, we structure projects to align with emerging compliance frameworks and international mechanisms, including Article 6 pathways.
