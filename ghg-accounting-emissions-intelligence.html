Capabilities

GHG Accounting & Emissions Intelligence

Quantify and translate emissions across operations and value chains into decision-grade insights for reporting and decarbonization.

Remedon supports organizations in turning emissions data into structured intelligence for reporting, strategy, and action. The work spans direct and indirect emissions across operations and value chains, helping teams understand sources, prioritize interventions, and build a stronger foundation for disclosure and decarbonization planning.

What we deliver

  • Scope 1, 2, 3 emissions accounting
  • Value chain (Scope 3) mapping & analysis
  • Lifecycle assessment (LCA)
  • Supplier, logistics, and downstream emissions modeling
  • Decarbonization pathways & target setting
